Vacation Policy

Full-time employees are entitled to 15 days of paid vacation per year.
Part-time employees receive vacation days on a pro-rata basis.
Vacation days must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused vacation days can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days per year.
Vacation pay is calculated based on your regular hourly rate.
Employees must have completed 90 days of employment before taking vacation.
