Parental Leave Policy

Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
Parental leave applies to biological, adoptive, and foster parents equally.
Employees must have worked at the company for at least 12 months to qualify.
Leave must begin within 12 months of the birth or adoption of a child.
Employees can take parental leave in one continuous block or in two separate periods.
